About Us


What is the Repository?
The Repository is a project dedicated to collecting and document the stories of people's experiences that could be explained by the supernatural. The repository is meant to be a resource for practical study. Eventually the Repository hopes to create a network of investigators and researchers documenting and organizing these stories.

Who created the Repository?
For our privacy, the brother & sibling team behind the Repository have elected not to share our actual identities at this time. Depending on how much this project grows, we may re-evaluate this decision later.
